Training Services

We have partnered with Kasperky to bring you the best Cyber Security courses for both technical and non technical personnel aimed at ensuring your network and systems are secure in the digital age.

Cyber Security Awareness Training

Increased social engineered attacks require organizations to keep raising cyber security awareness to their employees to prevent attacks that could evade the available security systems. We have designed an interactive cyber-awareness program that targets all users in an organization from the C-suite to operation staff.

Why choose this course?

  1. It is very interactive
  2. Easy to measure progress
  3. Use of simulated social engineering attacks
  4. Suitable for non IT personnel.
Advanced Cyber Security for IT Teams

The course is meant to equip IT security teams with skills to detect and respond to complex attacks. The attacks use unknown tactics, techniques and procedures which makes it difficult to detects. The course aims to help organizations build in-house capacity to detect, deter and manage cyber attack events.

Why choose this course?

  1. It is very interactive
  2. Easy to measure progress
  3. Use of various simulated cyber attacks
  4. Suitable for IT security personnel.
