IT Infrastructure

IT Infrastructure form the backbone of an organizations digital systems. We have partnered IT service and equipment industry leaders to offer you solutions that guarantee your availabilty 24/7.

Secure Network Design
Local Area Network (LAN)

We work with customers to design a highly available and secure local area Network (LAN) that allows users to access only those resources they are allowed to access. This design helps to reduce the impact of attacks by controlling lateral movement once an attacker gains access to the network.

Wireless Local Area Network(WLAN)

We work with you towards a cable free office setup by designing a highly available secure wireless network. We will help you to transition to WI-FI 7 that has high speeds compared to previous WI-FI standards.

Secure Software Defined Wide Area Network (SD-WAN)

We work with our partners Cisco and Fortinet to implement Secure SD-WAN to connect branch offices more securely and through optimized data flow without having to backhaul the HQ to access the internet. The design is also optimized for cloud workloads ensuring users can access resources faster and securely.

Data Centre − Server and Storage

We have partnered with Lenovo and Pure Storage to provide virtualized data center infrastructure that is secure, reliable and resilient from technical failures.

Telephony

We design and implement voice collaborations solutions, that helps you to work from anywhere, on premise and cloud hosted telephony solutions depending on client use case. In addition, we integrate the telephone solution with Microsoft TEAMs to further support employee mobility improving their productivity. We have partnered with major collaboration partners Cisco and Avaya to provide you with world class solutions.

Network Security (On premise and Cloud)

We help secure your Internal information assets from external threats through highly effective next generation firewall solutions. The firewall prevents against ransomware, phishing email, Spam emails, zero day attacks, malicious malware as well as blocking dangerous sites. We have partnered with Checkpoint a leading next generation firewall provider with solutions that cater for any business size.

Endpoint Security

Endpoint devices are one of the main targets for the attackers, a weakness in endpoint device can expose the entire organization. We have partnered with Kaspersky a leading security provider to provide an endpoint protection that highly effective against known and unknown attacks. The cloud solution provides protection for cloud workloads such as Microsoft office 365.

Microsoft Azure and Office 365 Administration

Are you in Azure cloud and office 365 and having challenges administering this platforms? We have qualified Microsoft cloud personnel who can assist in administering and help to maximize their benefits

Computer Sales
