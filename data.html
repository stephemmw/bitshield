Data Backup and Recovery

In collaboration with Veeam, we've crafted solutions ensuring seamless business continuity and robust data recovery in catastrophic scenarios. Protect your operations and data assets with our comprehensive disaster recovery solutions.

Data Backup and Replication

Our solution offers versatile data backup options, storing your data securely on hardware servers or leading cloud providers like Microsoft Azure, AWS, and Google Cloud. Benefit from real-time replication with minimal Recovery Point Objective (RPO) and Recovery Time Objective (RTO) of up to 5 minutes. Your backups are maintained in an immutable state, safeguarding them against ransomware attacks and ensuring data integrity.

Disaster Recovery Orchestration

Our solution facilitates automated Disaster Recovery (DR) processes, including DR drills and failover procedures. Enjoy seamless orchestration with the ability to sequence startup times for DR servers, ensuring efficient and reliable recovery operations. Simplify DR management and enhance readiness with our advanced automation features.
