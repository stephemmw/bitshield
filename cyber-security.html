Cyber Security Solutions

Modern organizations are frequently becoming targets of cyber attacks using new tactical, techniques and procedures (TTPs) to compromise security defense systems. With this in mind we partnered with leading cyber security experts to craft solutions to protect your systems and data from evasive and sophisticated cyber attacks.

Vulnerability Assessment/ Penetration Testing

Amidst dynamic cyber threats, we've partnered with leading cybersecurity experts to bolster your defenses. Our customized solutions safeguard your systems and data, providing robust protection against evolving threats. Secure your organization's future with confidence, partnering with us.

Identity Security

Enhance security with an additional layer for staff authentication and privileged accounts. Safeguard vital systems, even in the event of compromised usernames and passwords. Strengthen your organization's defenses with our comprehensive approach to account protection.

Database Activity Monitoring

Our database monitoring solution swiftly detects abnormal activities, like suspicious accounts accessing data at unusual times or from unfamiliar locations. Prompt alerts empower administrators to intervene swiftly, mitigating potential damage and maintaining data integrity effectively.

Endpoint Detection and Response

Teaming up with Kaspersky, we offer cutting-edge detection and response solutions. Detect and thwart even the most sophisticated attacks that bypass traditional security measures. Enhance your defense against evolving cyber threats with our advanced partnership.

Cloud Security

Experience unparalleled data security with our cloud security solutions. Leveraging Harmony, a Check Point cloud security solution, we safeguard your data stored in cloud platforms like Microsoft Office 365, Salesforce, and beyond. Protect your digital assets with confidence.
